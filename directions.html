PONG

How To Play:

To win the game you must be the first player or team to reach 10 points.


Need a bathroom break mid game, no problem you can pause the game by pressing the "p" button.


After the game is over you can hit the back to menu button on the bottom to change game mode or you can press spacebar to play again.


2 Player Directions - Player 1 uses "q" to go up and "a" to go down, player 2 up arrow to go up and down arrow to go down.


4 player Directions - This is team Pong, Where players 1 and 2 are together and 3 and 4 are on the other team. The controlls for player 1 is "q" to go up and "a" to go down. Player 2 "r" goes up and "f" goes down. For the other team player 3 "u" goes up and "j" goes down. Player 4 up arrow goes up and down arrow goes down.
